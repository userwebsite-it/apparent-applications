Low barrier entry

Students can join through beginner-friendly roles like idea collaborator, feedback helper, researcher, or outreach support.

Structured contribution

Projects use specific claimable tasks, limited spots, and founder verification so involvement is clear.

Application-ready proof

The goal is not empty titles. The goal is documented work, leadership, teamwork, and outcomes.
